NexusThread

Immutable AI Traceability

NexusThread creates a cryptographic audit trail for every single prediction. Never guess why a model made a decision—NexusThread maps the journey from training data to output.

  • Decision Lineage: Link every prediction back to the specific version of weights and training shards.
  • Weight Evolution: Track how model parameters shift over time in fine-tuned environments.
  • Input Reconstruction: Reproduce exact environment states for debugging edge-case failures.
Utility: Absolute regulatory compliance & zero-guess debugging
